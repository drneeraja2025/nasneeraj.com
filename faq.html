Frequently Asked Questions

üí∞ Pricing & Plans

What are the pricing options for SISLMS?
SISLMS offers flexible monthly pricing based on the number of students:
  • Starter: $30/month (1-25 students, 5GB storage)
  • Small: $60/month (26-50 students, 10GB storage)
  • Medium: $120/month (51-100 students, 20GB storage)
  • Large: $240/month (101-200 students, 40GB storage)
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing (201+ students, 80GB+ storage)
All plans include all features, unlimited users, and full support. Visit our pricing section for more details.
Are there any setup fees or hidden costs?
No, there are no setup fees or hidden costs. The monthly subscription includes:
  • Full platform access
  • All features and modules
  • Unlimited users and administrators
  • Email support
  • Regular updates and security patches
  • Data storage (as specified in your plan)
The only additional cost would be if you exceed your storage limit, in which case we'll discuss upgrade options.
Can I change my plan if my student count changes?
Yes, absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. If your student enrollment increases, simply contact us to upgrade to the appropriate tier. If enrollment decreases, you can downgrade at the start of your next billing cycle. We'll help ensure a smooth transition with no data loss.
Do you offer annual billing discounts?
Currently, we offer monthly billing for flexibility. However, we're happy to discuss annual billing options and potential discounts for schools that commit to longer terms. Please contact us to discuss custom pricing arrangements.
What happens if I exceed my student limit?
If you're approaching your student limit, we'll notify you in advance. You can either upgrade to the next tier or we can discuss a custom Enterprise plan that fits your exact needs. We'll work with you to find the best solution without disrupting your operations.

üîí Security & Compliance

Is SISLMS FERPA compliant?
Yes, SISLMS is designed to be FERPA (Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act) compliant. We implement:
  • Secure data encryption (in transit and at rest)
  • Role-based access controls
  • Audit logging of all data access
  • Secure authentication and authorization
  • Data retention policies aligned with FERPA requirements
For detailed information, see our Security & Compliance page.
Is SISLMS COPPA compliant?
Yes, SISLMS complies with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) requirements. We:
  • Require parental consent for students under 13
  • Implement strict data collection and usage policies
  • Provide parental access to student data
  • Maintain secure data handling practices
Our Privacy Policy details our COPPA compliance measures.
Does SISLMS meet WSDPA requirements for Wisconsin schools?
Yes, SISLMS is designed to meet Wisconsin Student Data Privacy Agreement (WSDPA) requirements. We provide:
  • WSDPA-compliant data handling
  • Clear data ownership policies
  • Secure data storage and transmission
  • Compliance with Wisconsin state requirements
We provide a WSDPA contract template that schools can use. SISLMS is Wisconsin Compliant, as indicated by our badge on the homepage.
Where is student data stored?
Student data is stored securely in cloud infrastructure provided by Supabase, which uses enterprise-grade data centers. All data is:
  • Encrypted at rest and in transit
  • Stored in secure, compliant data centers
  • Backed up regularly
  • Protected by multiple layers of security
We maintain SOC 2 Type 2 compliant infrastructure to ensure the highest security standards.
Who owns the student data?
Schools retain full ownership of all student data. NAS acts as a data processor, not a data owner. We:
  • Never sell or share student data with third parties
  • Only use data to provide the SISLMS service
  • Allow schools to export their data at any time
  • Delete data upon account termination (per retention policies)
This is detailed in our Terms of Service and WSDPA contract.
What happens to our data if we cancel?
Upon cancellation, you can:
  • Export all data: We'll provide a complete export of all student records, grades, and other data in standard formats (CSV, Excel, PDF)
  • Data retention: We retain data for 30 days after cancellation to allow for final exports
  • Permanent deletion: After the retention period, all data is permanently deleted from our systems
We'll work with you to ensure a smooth transition and complete data export before final deletion.
