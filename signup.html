Pricing Information

SISLMS offers flexible pricing based on your school size:

  • Essential: $2,000/year (up to 50 students)
  • Professional: $3,000/year (up to 150 students) - Recommended
  • Enterprise: $4,000/year (up to 200 students)
  • Unlimited: $5,000/year (unlimited students)

Special Discounts: Non-profit schools receive 20% off. School systems/districts receive volume discounts (10-25%). Early adopter discount: 10% for first 50 schools.
