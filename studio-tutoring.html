How Each Academy Type Uses Gurukul

Gurukul by NAS is tailored to meet the unique needs of different types of academies. Here's how each type leverages Gurukul's powerful features:

üéµ Music Academies

Gurukul helps music academies manage students, instructors, and instruments with specialized features designed for musical education:

  • Instrument Management: Track which students are learning which instruments (piano, violin, guitar, drums, etc.), manage instrument assignments, and track instrument condition and maintenance schedules.
  • Practice Time Logging: Students can log daily practice hours through the parent portal. Instructors can review practice time to gauge student commitment and adjust lesson plans accordingly.
  • Recital & Performance Tracking: Schedule recitals, competitions, and performances. Track student participation, assign pieces, store performance recordings, and maintain a performance history for each student.
  • Music Theory Progress: Track music theory lessons separately from practical lessons. Manage theory assignments, tests, and progress through different theory levels (ABRSM, Trinity, etc.).
  • Lesson Material Management: Assign sheet music, exercises, and practice materials digitally. Students and parents can access materials 24/7 through the portal.
  • Multi-Instructor Scheduling: Manage scheduling for multiple instructors teaching different instruments. Avoid double-booking instructors and optimize instructor availability.
  • Student Repertoire Tracking: Maintain a digital portfolio of pieces each student has learned, is currently learning, and plans to learn. Track mastery levels and technique progress.

üíÉ Dance Studios

Dance studios use Gurukul to manage classes, costumes, competitions, and progression through levels:

  • Dance Style & Level Tracking: Organize classes by dance style (ballet, hip-hop, contemporary, jazz, etc.) and track student progression through levels (beginner, intermediate, advanced, pre-professional).
  • Belt/Rank Progression System: For martial arts-based dance or traditional forms, track belt/rank progression. Set requirements for each level, track student progress toward next rank, and manage testing schedules.
  • Competition & Recital Management: Schedule competitions, recitals, and showcases. Register students for events, track registration fees, manage costumes and props, and store performance photos/videos.
  • Costume & Prop Management: Track costume sizes for each student, manage costume inventory, schedule fittings, and track costume condition and replacement needs.
  • Choreography & Rehearsal Notes: Store choreography notes, rehearsal schedules, and special instructions for each class. Students can access rehearsal videos and notes through the portal.
  • Flexibility & Technique Assessments: Track student progress in flexibility, technique, and dance-specific skills. Maintain assessment records to show improvement over time.
  • Performance Teams & Troupe Management: Manage performance teams and troupes separately from regular classes. Track team members, rehearsal schedules, and performance commitments.
  • Studio Space & Equipment: Manage studio rooms, mirrors, barres, sound systems, and other equipment. Schedule equipment usage and track maintenance needs.

üìö Private Tutoring Centers

Tutoring centers leverage Gurukul to track academic progress, manage sessions, and demonstrate student improvement:

  • Subject Area & Curriculum Tracking: Organize tutoring by subject (math, science, English, test prep, etc.) and track which curriculum each student follows (Common Core, IB, AP, state-specific standards).
  • Test Score Improvement Tracking: Record baseline test scores and track improvement over time. Generate progress reports showing score improvements, which helps demonstrate value to parents and improves retention.
  • Homework & Assignment Management: Assign homework through Gurukul, track completion rates, and allow students to submit work digitally. Parents can see assignments and completion status in real-time.
  • Academic Milestone Tracking: Track progress toward academic milestones (mastering multiplication, completing algebra, improving reading level, etc.). Celebrate achievements and set new goals.
  • One-on-One & Group Session Management: Schedule individual tutoring sessions and small group sessions. Manage tutor availability, match students with appropriate tutors, and optimize session scheduling.
  • Parent Conference Scheduling: Allow parents to schedule conferences through the portal. Store conference notes, action items, and follow-up tasks. Send automated reminders before conferences.
  • Learning Style & Accommodation Tracking: Record each student's learning style, accommodations needed (extra time, visual aids, etc.), and preferences. Help tutors personalize instruction.
  • Test Prep & Exam Scheduling: For test prep centers, manage exam registration (SAT, ACT, etc.), track practice test scores, schedule mock exams, and generate score improvement reports.
  • Progress Reporting to Schools: Generate professional progress reports that can be shared with students' regular schools, helping demonstrate the value of tutoring services.

üé® Other Specialized Academies

  • STEM Centers: Organize by subject (coding, robotics, engineering), track project completion, manage equipment and materials.
