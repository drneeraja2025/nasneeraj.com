Video Production Notes

Visual Style Guidelines

  • Color Scheme: Primary: #667eea (purple), Secondary: #764ba2 (dark purple), Accent: #28a745 (green for checkmarks)
  • Typography: Clean, modern sans-serif (Segoe UI or similar)
  • Animation Style: Smooth transitions, fade-ins, subtle zoom effects
  • Logo: NAS logo with "Neeraj's AI Software" tagline
  • Screenshots: Use actual SISLMS screenshots from the application

Voiceover Guidelines

  • Tone: Professional, confident, friendly, solution-focused
  • Pace: Moderate (not rushed, but not slow)
  • Emphasis: Highlight cost savings, security, local support, fast implementation
  • Pronunciation: "SISLMS" (S-I-S-L-M-S), "NAS" (N-A-S)

Music & Sound

  • Background Music: Upbeat, professional, modern (royalty-free)
  • Volume: Low enough not to distract from narration
  • Transitions: Subtle sound effects for slide transitions
  • Ending: Music fades out smoothly
